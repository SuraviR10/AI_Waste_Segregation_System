# Earth Character Images Setup

## Required Images

Please save your 3 Earth character images in this folder with these exact names:

1. **earth_sad.jpg** - The crying/sad Earth character (first image you showed)
2. **earth_happy.jpg** - The happy/excited Earth character (second image you showed)
3. **earth_thinking.jpg** - The thinking/confused Earth character (third image you showed)

## Image Specifications

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 500x500 pixels or larger
- The images should show the full Earth character with face and body

## How to Add

1. Save your 3 Earth character images
2. Rename them to match the names above
3. Copy them to this `images` folder
4. Run the app - the talking assistant will automatically use them!

## Fallback

If images are not found, the app will use emoji versions (🌍) as fallback.
